The O6 Fleet Disjunction was not release and should not be considered part of 
Allied Forces.  Due to an unfortunate film error, the rules "- Lasts for 2 
turns." was omitted from the card.  Any O6 Fleet Disjunctions which may have 
been accidentally circulated in Allied Forces should be considered to have 
errata stating that it lasts for only 2 turns.
