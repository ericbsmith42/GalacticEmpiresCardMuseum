These cards allow the use of Foreign, Exclusive, or Adapted Technology. In addition to these, all Bolaar, Vektrean, and Allied ships (Drone, Erodi, Pakta'don, Paraloid, Shon-ti, Tarra'ki, and Trochilidae) can use Adapted Technology; they may use Foreign Technology freely (only of the empire they're Allied with for the Allied Empires), and may use Exclusive Technology if they have a card which would allow them to use Foreign Technology.
