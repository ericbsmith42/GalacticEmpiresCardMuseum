Retrieve Card from Discard Pile

With the exception of the O10 Time Portal and L6 Black Market, none of these cards allows you to take any card from the top of the discard pile. All are specific to a type of card retreived.

  • A6 Breeder   monster cards
  • B8 Manufacturing Plant   equipment cards
  • B8 Shipyard   ship cards
  • C1 Luck Guy   luck cards
  • C1 Used Spaceship Dealer   ship cards
  • C2 Mad Chemist nbsp; illness cards
  • C3 Construction Team   base cards
  • C4 Mad Scientist   monster cards
  • C5 Cybernetic Engineer   automaton cards
  • C7 Warcraft Coordinator   shuttle or transporter
  • R/C9 Indirigan Chieftain - Doctor Ryd'ganev (promotional card)   crew cards
  • R/E7 Holographic Simulator   crew cards
  • F7 Field of Death
  • G5 Pirate Raid
  • I2 Fighter Installation   shuttle/fighter cards
  • I5 Bob's Used Spaceship Lot   ship cards
  • G10 Salvage Mission
  • R/L6 Distress Beacon   crew cards
  • L6 Black Market   any card
  • L9 Lost Fleet   ship cards
  • L9 Timeline Alteration
  • M7 Ghost Fleet   ships
  • R/O3 Metamorphosis   crew or monster cards
  • O6 Reinvestment   terrain
  • O6 Wreckage Survivors   crew cards
  • O10 General Quarters   crew cards
  • O10 Time Portal   any card
  • S4 Nagaridni Pirate Salvage Tug (promotional card) equipment cards
  • T7 System - Clydon System   warcraft
  • T7 System - Vektrea System   vektrean ships
  • T8 Planet - Custom Planet (promotional card)   opponent's discard pile
  • T8 System - Tufor System   mines
