Force Opponent to Discard from Hand

Note that a number of these cards only force the discard under very specific circumstances. This is especially true of many of the promotional terrain cards, which only force a discard when the terrain is destroyed. Fortunately, discard decks are not really a viable strategy all their own as in That Other Game™. Cards which discard other cards from the deck or from play are covered in separate sections.
