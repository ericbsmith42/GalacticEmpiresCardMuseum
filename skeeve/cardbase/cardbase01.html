Control Opponent's Ship

Taking control of opponent ships for extended amounts of time is an ability to peculiar to the Filarian Infesters. There are a few cards available which will allow you take control of ships for short amounts of time.

  • R/L4 Miscommunications   weapons fire only
  • M6 Mind Control Beast
  • R/O4 Mutiny Ship (promotional card)
  • O6 Bribe Pirate   pirate ship only
