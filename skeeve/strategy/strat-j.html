Empire Deck Strategy

J'xar

J'xar

Effective use of the transgate ability of the J'xar is critical to success with this empire. It is essential to use the E4 Transgate Stabilizer, E8 Transgate Stabilizer and O7 Intergalactic Transgate. Please note that when a ship uses a transgate in reaction to bring a jump ship into play, it is the transgate-equipped ship which is reacting, not the jump ship being played. Therefore, the jump ship cannot fire its weapons as a reaction.

This is an excellent empire for a "recycler" deck. In addition to the transgate ability, make use of Ted Peer's custom junkyard terrains, L9 Lost Fleet and anything else you can make work to keep your ships in play, in hand or out of the discard pile.

Key cards:

  • C3 J'xar Gravitic Engineer allows terrain to be called back to hand through transgates
  • C8 J'xar High Guard transgates operate for 1 less energy
  • E4 Transgate Stabilizer
  • E8 Transgate Stabilizer
  • T9 Planet - J'xar Transgalactic has a 3-point transgate
