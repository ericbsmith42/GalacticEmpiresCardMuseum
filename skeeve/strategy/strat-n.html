Nobles

The main problem that Noble decks has been the relatively low firepower of generic ships, particularly in heavy weapons. Slowly but surely, this is being corrected. The best ships out there right now are the various police ships, S3 White Ship, S5 Fictitious Ship, S5 Independant Pirate Ship, S7 Independant Pirate Ship, S7 Colony Ship S7 Trade Ship and S9 Colony Ship.

Key cards:

  • T6 Planet - Custom Planet "Katryn" (promotional card)
  • T7 Planet - Custom Planet "Personal World" (promotional card)

Consider these cards:

  • C2 Entrepeneur (promotional)
  • T7 Planet - Water World
