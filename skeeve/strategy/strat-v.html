Vektrean Mercenaries

The temptation with a Vektrean deck, as with a Bolaar deck, is to load up with economy-producing terrain. See my basic design tips at the top of this page for some reasons why this is a Bad Thing. As with the Bolaar, using adapated technology is to your advantage (see the Bolaar section for details on this as well).

Key cards:

  • T4 Planet - Vektrea Minor
  • T7 System - Vektrea System

Card Combinations for Vektrean Mercenaries

Fast Ships

by David S. Auer

S2 Patrol Ship + E9 Hologram
Use this combo to place a ship into play just before your turn begins, allowing you engage the ship on your turn.
