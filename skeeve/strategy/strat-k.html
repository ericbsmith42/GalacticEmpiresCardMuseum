Krebiz Capitalist Alliance

Krebiz Capitalist Alliance

I haven't played this empire very much, so I don't really have anything to offer at this point. Note that under the Universe edition card set and rules you can stock ship.capsule combos in your deck as single cards and play them as either the ship, capsule or the combined unit. It is also possible to hold a stack of Alpha/Beta/Primary ship and capsule cards to the side of the play area and use them during the game to represent these once they are brought into play.

Cards to consider:

  • T7 Planet - Krebiz Homeland doubles the effectiveness of armor in the fleet
  • T7 Planet - Krebizar capsules in the fleet repair 1 extra shield point per turn
