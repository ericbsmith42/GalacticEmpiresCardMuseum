Single Card Strategies

L8 Frayed Time Spindle: "Annoying thing about frayed time spindles, some of the best stacks aren't strong enough. If you play a card or two against the stack, you can make it frayable. When you play a T3 Star - Antimatter Star against a terrain stack, the terrain (and consequently its stack) gets 3 points bigger. An H8 Quasi-particle makes the stack 8 bigger. The added 11 points should make most any terrain stack spindle-bait, with the added bonus that the opponent must discard half of the terrain's strength (which is the original strength + 3) number of cards from his hand."

- with thanks to David S. Auer

P8 Psychotic Commandant: "Opponent fires at shielded target. Transport the commandant over and half damage to shields has been effectively prevented (this is not damage reduction mind you, the commander foils even E10 Dark Circuitry). Protect allies [other players] with a there-and-back mission." And try A6 Promotions and other function-doubling effects for even more fun and protection.

- with thanks to David S. Auer
