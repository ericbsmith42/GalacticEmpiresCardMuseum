Promotional Agenda Cards

G1 Spatial Manipulation
Hidden: When a ship is moved against a terrain in the fleet, move the ship to any terrain in an opponent fleet.
Exposed: Whenever a ship moves against a terrain in your fleet it takes damage equal to the strength of this card.
Available from Cards Un-LTD (908)272-9572

G2 Prized Females
Hidden: When 3 Indirigan Females or 2 Indirigan Females and a Chieftan or Sub-Chieftan are on an Indirigan ship in the fleet and are all under your control: every crew in the fleet capable of capturing crew does not require transportation to capture crew.
Available by winning an Indirigan Touring Game tournament

G7 Collect Things
{Collector}
Exposed: Accumulate 7 cards under anu Collector class card (Aesthetic class cards count for two each). Use those cards as if they were in play in your fleet for 1 player turn, then discard them to their owner's Discard Pile.
Available from Cards4U, Inc. (703)790-8048

G8 Aesthetic Balance
Hidden: If none of the cards in your fleet are the basis of a stack, reduce any 1 stack in play by one card (not the basis of a stack.)
Exposed: If none of your terrain are the basis of a stack, reduce any 1 terrain stack of cards bye one card (not the basis of a stack), discarding it.
- Discarded after use.
Available from Mint Card Co. (410)766-8071
