Promotional Equipment Cards

E1 Dark Hand Patrol Fighter
has 2 shields
- May be played independantly, protecting the Sector HQ.
- When discarded, return to the hand.
- Each turn, may fire its weapons or transport up to 1 crew from 1 location to another or on a there-and-back mission.
1 Specialized Weapon Mount; 1 Phaser
Available from Bill Murray (602)969-4674

R/E1 Emergency Shields
provides 3 Shields {P}
- Add 3 Shields to the unit.
- Ships are limited to 3 extra shields.
- Repair costs for the unit's shields are doubled.
- During the Engagement Phase, discard this card if the unit's shields are damaged.
Available from Anthony Medici (201)882-1714 (web site)

E1 Paint Job
{P; Aesthetic}
- Becomes an Aesthetic class card.
- All Collector class targets must be eliminated before this unit can fire at other targets.
- Increases a unit's strength by 1.
Available from Mint Card Co. (410)766-8071

E2 Battle Bird "Vulture Series"
{P}
- Played independently or to an eyrie.
- Each turn, may kill (discard) 1 crew that generates research.
- Damaged by research points.
Available from Silver Eagle Wargame Supplies

R/E2 Intruder Alert System
- Played to a ship or base.
- Negates any crew assaults to the location on which it is played.
- Discarded during owner's next Engagement Phaser.

E4 Battle Bird "Owl Series"
requires 1 supply {NP}
- Played independently or to an eyrie.
- Removes 1 patrol ship or independent shuttle fighter from the game.
- Damaged by research.
Available from Silver Eagle Wargame Supplies

R/E5 Aesthetic Fighter

- Transport 0 to 3 crew from one location to another.

- Fire its weapons.
1 Aesthetic Weapon; 1 Phaser
Available from Mint Card Co. (410)766-8071

E5 Battle Bird "Falcon Series"

- Damaged by research points.

R/E5 Cargo Transporter

- May be used by up to 3 automatons at location for a there-and-back mission.
Available from Anthony Medici (201)882-1714 (web site)

E6 Control Matrix
- Weapons which are ready, but have not fired, may be fired as a reaction.
- Crew may not control this unit, reduce damage caused to the unit, or modify damage caused by this unit.
- Luck cards may not be played to or against this unit.
2 Maneuvering Systems
Distributed in Companion Games' Playing Mat.

E6 Heavy Cargo Packs
- Played with 6 stored supply points.
- When on an Indirigan ship:
- Excess supply up to the strength of this card may be stored for use on your next turn. If not used it is lost.
- As a reaction, stored supply may be expended to prevent damage with each stored point negating one point of damage.
Available by winning an Indirigan Touring Game tournament

R/E6 Taxi Shuttle
{P}
- Each turn, as a reaction to a location with an opponent crew being destroyed, move 1 opponent crew to a location in their fleet and steal economy (for next allocation) from that opponent equal to the crew's strength. May not be used if opponent doesn't have enough economy.
Available from Mint Card Co. (410)766-8071

3 Phasers

E7 Collector's Storage Case
{P; Collector}
- Each turn, as a reaction to an opponent's card being discarded, move that card to the Collector's storage case (card is out of play).
- Opponent's may purchase their cards back (moving them to the Discard Pile) by allocating a numerb of economy equal to the strength of the card purchased.
Available from Cards4U, Inc. (703)790-8048

E7 Megasonic Phase Distorter
