Promotional Luck Cards

L1 Collector's Artifact
requires 2 Research {Collector}
- Played to a terrain card.
- Each turn, look at the top 3 cards on the deck. Optionally, rearrange the 3 cards, discarding this card.
Available from Cards4U, Inc. (703)790-8048 Note: this card is misprinted as being available from Wizard's Workshop.

L5 Artifact - Galactic Paintbrush
{Aesthetic}
May do 1 of the following:
- Move 1 card from one stack to another in an opponent fleet.
- Discard a Collector class card on play (not the basis of a stack).
- Discarded after use.
Available from Mint Card Co. (410)766-8071

L6 Black Market
- Played during the Allocation Phase.
- Allows Player to go through his discard pile and retrieve one card of 5 or less strength.
- This costs a number of economy points equal to the strength of the retrieved card.
- Discarded after use.

L6 Shuttle Bay Explosion
May be played on a ship or base.
- All shuttecraft and fighters on the ship or base are destroyed.
- Shuttlecraft and fighters may not be played in reaction mode to the unit for one complete turn after this card is played.
- Score 1 damage point on the ship/base for each shuttle.fighter destroyed.

L7 Artifact - Collector Toy
{Collector}
- Played to or against a location.
- Prevents hazard or luck cards from affecting the location.
- Each turn, during the Allocation Phase, move this card to the highest strength location stack in play in any fleet.
Available from Cards4U, Inc. (703)790-8048

L7 Unfortunate Accident
- Played against a standard target.
- Causes 3 points of structural damage to the target (may damage terrain).
- Discarded after use.
Available from John Perrault

L8 Altered Timeline
- When played, one card from each opponent's active fleet is returned to their hands. Card is chosen by the player of this card.
- Card chosen must the last card played to its stack.
- Discarded after use.
