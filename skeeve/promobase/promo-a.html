Promotional Ability Cards

A1 Aestheticism
{Aesthetic}
- Played to a terrain, crew or dragon.
- The terrain, crew or dragon becomes an Aesthetic class card.
- All Collector class targets must be eliminated before the affected card can fire at other targets.
Available from Mint Card Co. (410)766-8071

A2 Scientific Knowledge
generates 1 Research
[-Played to a crew or dragon.]
- Each turn may negate the effect of any one hostile monster at his location.

R/A3 Adaptive Programming
- Played to a battle bird.
- 2 less damage from opponent application of research.
Available from Silver Eagle Wargame Supplies

R/A3 Emergency Medical Technician
- Increases the strength of a doctor by 2 points.
- When played in reaction mode, delays an illness, injury or plague card from taking effect for one turn.
Distributed in Cantrip magazine

A3 Pilot Background
[- Played to a crew card.]
- As a reaction, that crew can flee a unit being destroyed in a shuttle craft or fighter; (immediately moves to another location when unit is destroyed).

A5 Communications Training
generates 1 Command Point
- Played to a crew card.
- After player has drawn cards in the Draw Cards Phase, allows the player to view the next card in his deck.

A5 Enhanced Robotics
- Played to a battle bird.
- Increases the strength of the battle bird by 2 points.
Available from Silver Eagle Wargame Supplies

A5 Wizard's Ring
- Played to a non-aesthetic crew.
- Increases crew's strength by 2.
- During the Draw Phase, allows one extra card to be drawn.
- One card from the hand us then discarded.
Available from Wizard's Workshop (410)551-9236

A6 Dragon Marksman
- Played to a dragon
- Multiplies the damage done by the dragon by 1.5 (drop fractions).
Available from Mint Card Co. (410)766-8071

A6 Security Background
generates 1 Command point
- Prevents crew cards of equal or lesser strength from atatcking anything at his location except himself.

A10 Heavy Artillery

A10 Urban Influence
- Each turn, disengage an opponent location (with no crew present) for one complete turn via there-and-back mission.
Distributed in Galactic Fire #2
