Promotional Occurence Cards

R/O2 Partial Regeneration
- Played to the fleet.
- Generates 2 repair point[s] or healing point[s] for immediate repair of currently occuring or previous damage, on any standard target, including a Sector HQ or Psy Network.
- Discarded after use.
Available from Dan Gosselin Windows / C++ Consultant

R/O3 Collateral Damage
- Played against a card about to take structural damage.
- Discard[s] any 1 card in play on the affcted card (may not discard a unit or psy).
Available from John Perreault

R/O3 Military Reservations
- Played against an opponent crew.
- Move that crew to any comedy club.
Available from Gerry Smit

R/O4 Convoy Escort
- Must be played on an empire ship.
- As a reaction, that ship can intercept damage applied to any generic ship, taking two points of damage for each point intercepted.

O4 Mutiny Ship
- May only be played on an opponent ship of equal or lesser strength.
- Ship is controlled by mutiny player until destroyed or until owner has played cards generating a total of two command points to the ship.
- Discarded after use.
Distributed in Cards Illustrated magazine

R/O4 Strategic Deployment

O5 Freak Occurence
-Played during the Weapons Fire Phase.
- Allows a card to be played or a card in play to perform a function as though it were a Play Cards Phase.
- If used on a crew, the crew may use transportation at location.
Available from David Van Cleef

O5 Galactic Convention
- 1 crew card in each fleet (chosen by owner) is moved to the location of the convention and cannot be used for one complete turn.
- Discarded during allocation. The convention player receives 1 economy point from each opponent (subtracted from next allocation).
Distributed at GenCon 1995.

O5 Sudden Migration
- Played against an occumbus of equal or lesser strength.
- All ships in the occumbus fleet take damage equal to the strength of the occumbus, discarding the occumbus.
- Discarded after use.
Available from Andre McInnis (416)483-5385

O6 Empires in Conflict
- Played on two opponent fleets.
- The main empire ships in those fleets can only fire at the other affected fleet.
- May not be played if only 2 players remain in the game.
- Lasts for one complete turn and then is discarded.
Distributed in Scrye magazine

R/O6 Military Reservations
- Played against a ship in a Comedy Club Network player's fleet.
- That ship cannot fore for one turn.
- Discarded after use.
Available from Gerry Smit

O7 Grand Chieftain's Right of Tribe
{Persona}
- May exchange control of an Indirigan ship in the fleet with an Indirigan ship in an opponent's fleet.
- Original ship control is returned if another exchange can be made (through use of this card) or if this card leaves play.
Available by winning an Indirigan Touring Game tournament

O10 Empires in Conflict
- Played on two opponent fleets.
- All ships in those fleets can only fire at the other affected fleet.
- May not be played if only 2 players remain in the game.
- Lasts for one complete turn and then is discarded.
Distributed in Scrye magazine
