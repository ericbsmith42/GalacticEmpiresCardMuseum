Promotional Field Cards

T/F2 Access Field
produces 3 Repair
- Played to terrain, combining with it.
- Played and damaged as a terrain. May be the basis of a stack as a terrain. It is treated as a field card for all other purposes.
- Repair points generated by this terrain card can repair card damage.
Discipline: Cyber
3 - Remove or generate (and apply) up to 3 research points to any card in any fleet.
5 - Repair up to 3 damage or cause 3 psy damage to a ship, base or monster in any fleet.
Available from Anthony Medici (201)882-1714 (web site)

T/F5 Positron Field
requires 1 Energy, 1 Research; produces 4 Repair; has 5 Shield
- Played and damaged as a terrain. May be the basis of a stack as a terrain. It is treated as field card for all other purposes.
- Repair points generated by this terrain can repair card damage.
Discipline: Cyber
2 - Reduce the strength of the EMF on any one unit to zero.
6 - Causes 3 points of psy damage to the structure of one unit.
Available from Anthony Medici (201)882-1714 (web site)
