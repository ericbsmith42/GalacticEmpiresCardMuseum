Promotional Hazard Cards

R/H2 Time Trap
- Played on any card.
- The card stack is considered out of play.
- Discarded by applied research equal to its strength.
- May only be played in reaction mode when played to the fleet.
- Discarded after use.
Available from Elmer Lyons

H3 Divergent Anomoly
- Causes one ship on which it is played to be incapable of protecting its Sector HQ for 3 complete turns.
- Discarded after use.
Available from Anthony Medici (201)882-1714 (web site)

H6 Auer's Isotope
- Played against a card.
- Each turn, cause 1 structural damage to the card (even if it is not subject to damage; may damage terrain).
- This damage may not be repaired while this card is in play on the affected card (even if this card is disengaged).
- 6 research applied to this card will disengage this card for 1 complete turn.
Available from David S. Auer (908)671-8327

H6 Subspace Compression
- Played against a fleet.
- Damage equal to 1 point for each card in the opponent's hand above 12 is divided evenly between any number of standard targets in their fleet (may damage terrain).
Available from Gopin's Grenadiers (908)308-1789 (Alan Gopin)

R/H7 Ko's Dense Matter Nebula
- Played to a terrain.
- Prevents opponents from viewing your hand. Also prevents you from viewing an opponent's hand.
- Prevents an opponent from causing you to discard cards from your hand if you have 9 or fewer cads in your hand.
Available from David S. Auer (908)671-8327

H7 Time Tremor

H9 Star Dance
- Played against a star or system.
- Causes damage equal to the strength of the star or system to one other terrain in the same fleet.
- Discarded after use.
Available from Mint Card Co. (410)766-8071

H10 Cosmic Cataclysm
- Played against a fleet
- Remove all cards in that opponent's Discard Pile from the game.
- Removed from the game after use.
Available from Gopin's Grenadiers (908)308-1789 (Alan Gopin)
