Promotional Sector HQ Upgrade Cards

Q1 Patrol Ship Factory
- Played to the Sector HQ or Psy Network.
- Whenever a patrol ship you own is discarded, it may be placed in the Discard Pile or returned to your hand.
- Each turn, one patrol ship may be played that doesn't count toward your card play limit.
Distributed in Galactic Fire #3

Q2 Hydroponics Facility
- All cards in the fleet require one less supply to engage.
- Excess supply up to the strength of this card may be stored for use on the following turn, if not used it is then lost.
- One Indirigan ship in the fleet may engage for no supply cost.
Available by winning an Indirigan Touring Game tournament

Q5 Long Range Transporter
- Transporters in the fleet may transport crew to or from this card. Crew on this card are considered out of play for all purposes except for being transported off this card.
Available from Mint Card Co. (410)766-8071
