Promotional Crew Cards

C1 Opportunistic Rogue
requires 1 Economy
- May use one strength one function of any discipline in play, once each turn.

C1 Portrait Artiste
- Each turn, disengage for 1 complete turn any one opponent crew that generates at least one command point.
Available from Mint Card Co. (410)766-8071

C2 Entrepeneur
modifier -1 Economy
- When in play on an engaged [generic] ship, all opponents must pay their first economy point to the entrepeneur player for use on his next turn.
Distributed in Combo magazine

C3 Aerospace Procurement Officer
- One shuttlecraft may be played to location without using a Card Play.
- If in play on a carrier, an additional shuttlecraft may be played to location without using a Card Play.
Available from David van Cleef

C3 Time Travelling Arms Dealer
- Played to a terrain card which generates ammunition points.
- All ships in the fleet receive one free ammunition point each turn.
- A maximum of one such card may be in play in a fleet at any one time.
Distributed in Conjure magazine

R/C1 Avarian Mechanic
provides 2 Repair
- Repairs 2 points of damage to any battle bird each turn. When played in reaction, may be used to repair currently occuring damage.
- When in play on an eyrie can completely repair one battle bird at location.
Available from Silver Eagle Wargame Supplies

R/C4 Engineering Corps
provides 2 Repair
- The engineering corps can only function when played on terrain.
- Can repair up to two points of any type of damage scored on the terrain card each turn.
- Can repair card damage.
Distributed in Shadis magazine

C4 Super Genius
{Persona}
- Doubles the research output of location.
- When in play on a ship, allows the use of foreign technology.
- Any crew at location may be discarded instead of discarding this crew.
Available from Days of Knights (302)366-0963

C4 War Veteran
provides 1 Command point
- All crew cards at his location are treated as if they were 1 strength point bigger.
- If played on terrain, the supply point output is increased by 1 point.
- Cannot be killed by boarding parties or marines.
Awarded to winners of sanctioned tournaments.

C5 Archaeologist
provides 3 Research
- Allows the benefits of any artifact card in play to be used by his fleet also.
- Doubles the point output of any type of ruins card.
Available from Berkeley North Distributors

R/C5 Exogeologist
- May only function when played on a terrain card.
- Terrain may not be destroyed while the exogeologist is engaged and functioning. It may be reduced to a minimum of 1 strength point.
Distributed in Cantrip magazine

C5 HQ Repair Team
requires 1 Command Point; provides 1 Repair {Persona}
- Ability cards may not be played on this card.
- The repair point generated by this crew may be used t repair one point of damage to the Sector HQ.
Available from Neal Feldman (503)581-8335.

C5 Indirigan Female
- If captured the Female functions as if she belonged to the capturing player.
- All bases may not fire at the ship on which she is located.
- All Bi-Tritium Boomerang weapons may not fire on her ship.
Available by winning an Indirigan Touring Game tournament

C5 Indirigan Sub-chieftain - Semitsap
{Persona} modifer -2 Damage, -2 Supply
When on a Meerkats tribe ship:
- 2 less damage each opponent volley at location.
- 2 less supply required by the fleet.
- Increases the strength of cyber mages at location by 2.
Available from Pastimes (717)838-9502 at 1 East Main Street, Palmyra PA 17078

R/C5 Marine NCO
- May accompany another crew on a there-and-back mission.
- Adds 2 strength to the crew.
- Adds 2 damage to any damage caused by the crew.
- If the crew is discarded during the mission, this card is discarded instead.
Available from Alber A. Sidaras (914)386-1534.

C5 Statistician
- The player with the highest strength statistician (no ties) may, as a reaction, alter 1 marker die being placed (or altered) by 1 point (Maximum of 6, Minimum of 1).
- May not affect the same die more than once.
Available from Elmer Lyons

C5 War Dog
- All war dogs at location must be eliminated before other crew may be affected by crew attacks.

C6 Super Genius - Brilliant G.E.M.
{Persona}
- Doubles the research output of location stack.
- Allows the use of foreign technology at location.
- Any crew at location may be discarded instead of discarding this card.
Available from Days of Knights (302)366-0963

C7 The Collector
{Collector}
- Each turn, the lowest strength card which is not the basis of a stack, in play in any fleet is placed underneath this card out of play.
- If this card is dsicarded, the controlloing player receives 1 economy point per card collected for use during their next Allocation Phase. All collected cards are discarded.
Available from Cards4U, Inc. (703)790-8048
